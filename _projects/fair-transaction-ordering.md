This project studied transaction order fairness in DAG-based systems, with a focus on how distributed protocols can reason about ordering when participants observe transactions at different times.

The work connects distributed algorithms, blockchain protocol design, and adversarial ordering behavior. It was completed as my M.Sc. thesis at Delft University of Technology.
